# Finance Document Governance Policy

Finance reports are classified as internal, confidential, or restricted based on business sensitivity.

Internal finance dashboards may be accessed by employees with standard corporate identity access.

Confidential finance reports, including forecasts, budget planning documents, acquisition analysis, and non-public revenue commentary, may be accessed only by Finance leadership, approved executives, and authorized audit reviewers.

AI-generated answers based on finance documents must be grounded in approved internal sources and must include citations to the retrieved source chunks.

If retrieval confidence is low, the system must withhold the answer or clearly state that the available documents do not contain enough information.
