# HR Access and Leave Policy

This internal policy describes how employee HR documents are accessed and governed.

Employees may access general HR policies, benefits information, and leave guidelines through the internal knowledge portal.

Confidential HR case files are restricted to HR Business Partners and authorized Legal reviewers. Managers may view only the employee records required for their direct reporting responsibilities.

Parental leave requests require manager notification and HR approval. The current internal policy states that primary caregivers may request up to sixteen weeks of paid parental leave, subject to employment eligibility and documentation review.

All HR policy answers generated by AI systems must cite the source policy section used to produce the answer.
